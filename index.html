WHAT DOES THE PROGRAMMING TEAM DO?

Competitions

We compete in challenges as a team, including the Hackathons and Programming Competitions.

Learn To Code

If you’re looking to get familiar with programming, we hold sessions to teach you to code and give you all the resources to continue in your own time.

Speakers

Throughout the year, we hold presentations from industry professionals and academics. Our keynote speaker last year was Michael Litman, a Brown professor who presented his work on Machine Learning.
